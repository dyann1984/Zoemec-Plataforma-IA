# ZOEMEC AI Demo

Demo URL:

https://zoemec-plataforma-ia.vercel.app

## 2-3 Minute Demo Script

1. Sign in.
   - Use Google authentication or create a new account.
   - Do not use or publish personal credentials.

2. Open **Generar APU**.
   - Enter a real construction concept, for example a wall, electrical output, concrete element or finishing work.

3. Generate the APU.
   - Show how ZOEMEC classifies the concept.
   - Review materials, labor, equipment, FSR, indirect costs and utility.

4. Ask ZOE for context.
   - Ask ZOE to explain the matrix.
   - Ask for incoherences or validation notes.

5. Edit a value.
   - Change a material price, labor factor or indirect percentage.
   - Show that the unit price recalculates.

6. Add to budget.
   - Send the approved APU into the budget module.

7. Export deliverables.
   - Download PDF.
   - Download XLSX.

## What Judges Should Notice

- ZOEMEC is not a generic dashboard.
- It is organized around the technical workflow from document to deliverable.
- AI is used as a validation and explanation layer, not as blind automation.
- The output remains editable and auditable by a cost engineer.

## Safe Demo Guidance

- Do not show private `.env` values.
- Do not show service-account JSON.
- Do not use real client confidential data.
- Use representative construction concepts and sanitized technical files.
